PARTICIPANTS NEEDED

To participate in experiments on the project

Pedestrian interaction with vibrating slender structures


We are looking for:

VOLUNTEERS*, MAN and WOMEN, of any age

who are students or staff at the University of Exeter


YOU WILL BE ASKED TO:

walk on a treadmill placed on a platform (left) and over a wobbly bridge (right)


YOUR DATA WILL HELP STRUCTURAL ENGINEERS:

to design environmentally and human-friendly, comfortable bridges


More information can be found in this website under vPERFORM category.

(* to be eligible to take part in the tests you should not be suffering from motion sickness, vibration sensitivity and/or locomotion impairment)
